Most Viewed YouTube Videos

Who are we?
We’re a data-driven team on a mission to track and showcase the most viewed YouTube videos around the world — updated every 4 to 6 hours. Whether MrBeast has dropped another crazy challenge, BLACKPINK released a new MV, or T-Series hit another billion, we’re already listing it.

What’s our goal?
To provide real-time, reliable, and refreshingly simple access to YouTube’s trending landscape. Currently, we’re global — but our dream is to go local. Imagine being able to filter videos by country and ask, “What was the most viewed video in the U.S. in the last 24 hours?” Yeah, we want that too.

We’re not begging for funds, but let’s just say... support helps. The more we grow, the faster we’ll deliver features like country-based filters, historical trend tracking, and more.

Until then, enjoy the latest from Taylor Swift, PewDiePie, Bad Bunny, Alan Walker, Zach King, KSI, Selena Gomez, and many more — served fresh, with stats.
